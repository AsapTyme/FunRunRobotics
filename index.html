About Us

The Fort Hays Tech Northwest Robotics Club was founded in 2023 to provide students with hands-on experience in robotics. We're dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence in the field of robotics engineering.

Our club welcomes students of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced engineers, providing a supportive environment where creativity and technical skills can flourish.

Our Activities

Competitions

We participate in regional and national robotics competitions, giving students real-world experience in designing, building, and programming competitive robots.

Workshops

Regular sessions on programming and engineering fundamentals, helping members develop the skills needed for successful robotics projects.

Community Projects

We develop robotics solutions for local community needs, applying our technical knowledge to solve real-world problems.

Join Our Club
